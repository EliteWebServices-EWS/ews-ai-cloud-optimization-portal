About Elite Web Services

A cloud-native consulting firm helping businesses modernize, scale, and innovate on AWS — combining strategy, architecture, and operations into one engagement.

Who We Are

Elite Web Services is a cloud-native consulting firm focused on helping businesses modernize, scale, and innovate using Amazon Web Services. As an emerging AWS Partner, we provide tailored cloud solutions that align with customer goals, combining strategy, architecture, and operations into one engagement — rather than handing clients off between disconnected teams.

Our Mission

We architect, automate, and manage cloud environments on AWS that drive innovation, improve performance, and reduce operational complexity for our clients. Our vision is to become the go-to AWS cloud services partner for small and mid-market enterprises, delivering transformative, secure, and sustainable digital solutions.

How We Work

Assess

Cloud strategy and readiness assessments, plus AWS Well-Architected Framework reviews, before any work begins.

Migrate & Build

Rehost, replatform, or refactor — paired with security, compliance, and DevOps/CI-CD automation built in from the start.

Optimize

Ongoing cloud cost optimization (FinOps) and data & analytics solutions to keep environments efficient as they grow.

Support

Managed cloud services and support after go-live — we stay accountable for the environment, not just the handoff.

Team Roles

Every engagement is staffed by AWS-certified specialists across the disciplines a real cloud transformation needs:

Cloud Architect

Owns the Well-Architected Framework review, target architecture, and migration strategy.

Security & Compliance Lead

Builds HIPAA, SOC 2, and financial-regulation requirements into the environment from day one.

DevOps Engineer

Implements CI/CD automation and infrastructure-as-code so environments stay reproducible.

FinOps / Cost Analyst

Monitors and tunes cloud spend on an ongoing basis as part of managed services.
