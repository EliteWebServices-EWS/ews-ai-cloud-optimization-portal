Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 — Elite Web Services LLC

What we collect

When you contact us or request an assessment, we collect your name, business email, phone number, and company information. We may also collect non-sensitive technical details required to perform cloud assessments (such as publicly provided architecture summaries).

How we use data

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, provide assessments, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data. Any data provided for cloud assessments is handled with strict least-privilege access and never stored longer than necessary for the engagement.

Security

We follow standard security practices including encryption in transit, role-based access, and strict credential handling. For customer cloud access we recommend using cross-account IAM roles with limited read-only permissions.
